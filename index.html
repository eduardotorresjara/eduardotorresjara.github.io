Short Bio:

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Robotics Engineering Program at the Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) and the founder director of the Sensitive Robotics Laboratory. In 2007, I finished my PhD at MIT. Then, I was a postdoc in the Distributed Robotics and the Humanoid Robotics Laboratories at CSAIL as well as in the Microrobotics Laboratory at Harvard.
